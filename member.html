Become a Member

Protect our planet and enjoy free admission, valuable savings, and exclusive events!

Members at all levels enjoy these benefits::

  • Discounted admission to Garden Glow, festivals, and other signature events
  • A set of personalized printed membership cards (with a digital membership card option)
  • Exclusive previews and early entry to floral shows, plant sales, and signature events
  • Reciprocal privileges at 300+ gardens across North America
  • Passes for free tram tours and Children’s Garden entry (unlimited access at higher levels)
  • Invitations to members-only events
  • 10% discount at all retail locations (does not apply to sale merchandise)
  • Valuable savings on education classes, workshops, and family programs
  • Free subscription to the Bulletin, member e-news, and education catalog

The President's Society:

Increase your investment and involvement in the Garden and enjoy exclusive privileges as a member of the Garden Giving Societies.

PRESIDENT’S CONTRIBUTOR

All General Membership benefits, plus:

  • Admission privileges for a total of 8 adults and any children 12 and younger
  • 8 complimentary passes to Garden Glow, festivals, and selected exhibits (no timed tickets required!)
  • Invitation for 2 to Garden Glow and Orchid Show Donor Previews
  • Invitation for 2 to annual President’s Society Reception
  • Cuttings, the President’s quarterly newsletter
  • Invitations to exclusive Spotlight Series events and additional learning programs

  • A complimentary Missouri Botanical Garden wall calendar (mailed in the fall)
